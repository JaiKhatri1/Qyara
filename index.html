FAQ

Questions we get all the time

Everything you need to know before making the switch.

Does Qyara use my data to train other bots? ▾
Absolutely not. Your data is private, isolated, and encrypted. Qyara's AI is trained exclusively on your uploaded documents — not shared with any other customers or used to train any base models. Your knowledge base belongs only to you.
What file types can I upload? ▾
Qyara accepts PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV, XLSX, Markdown, and plain HTML files. You can also paste text directly, add a website URL to crawl, or connect a help center like Zendesk, Notion, or Intercom via our integrations.
How accurate is the chatbot? ▾
On domain-specific queries within your uploaded content, Qyara achieves 98.6% accuracy in controlled testing. When a question falls outside its training scope, it's configured to gracefully escalate — never hallucinate. You control the fallback behavior.
Can I white-label it and resell to clients? ▾
Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes full white-label capabilities. You get a custom domain, remove all Qyara branding, apply your own logo and color scheme, and charge clients whatever you want. Many agencies use Qyara as a core part of their service offering.
What if customers ask something the bot doesn't know? ▾
You can configure a custom fallback action: show a message, collect an email, open a live chat window, or send an alert to your team. The bot will never make up an answer — it'll tell the user it doesn't know and guide them to the right place.
How do I embed Qyara on my website? ▾
Copy one line of JavaScript and paste it before your closing </body> tag. That's it. It works with Webflow, Shopify, WordPress, Wix, custom React or Vue apps — anywhere you can add a script tag. Our dashboard also provides a shareable hosted URL if you prefer not to embed.
Is there a free trial? ▾
Yes. Every plan includes a 14-day full-featured free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll have access to all features during the trial — we want you to see real results before committing to anything.
What languages does Qyara support? ▾
Qyara supports 50+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Portuguese, Hindi, and many more. Language detection is automatic — no configuration needed. The bot will respond in whatever language the user writes in.
